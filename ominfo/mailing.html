OM Mailing List

Converse daily with OMers across the country with the Odyssey Mailing List. A mailing list, also know as a listserv or e-mail exploder, is a program that resends e-mail sent to it to a group of subscribers. It's like a newsgroup, but more private, and easier to use, as you can use your ordinary e-mail software.

To subscribe, send a message to the address

  • odyssey-request@vombo.org

with the following on the first line:

  • subscribe

After you are subscribed, any e-mails you send to the address codyssey@vombo.org will be forwarded to all of the other subscribers. Likewise, you will receive message they send to that address.
If you don't send us e-mail, we'll never know how many people are using the site!

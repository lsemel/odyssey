Profiles of OMers Active on the Internet

If you would like to add your name to this list, contact Lee Semel and include some brief information about yourself, your involvement, as well as your e-mail address. You may also include a scanned photograph of yourself.

Lee Semel maintains the Edison, NJ OM Creativity Site at http://mars.superlink.net/~lsemel, and volunteers for OM in a variety of ways. He has been involved in OM for more than ten years. He is a recent graduate of Rutgers University where he studied computer science and art history, and currently designs Web sites. He currently lives in Edison, New Jersey, a town tolerable only because of its proximity to New York City.

Matthew Semel maintains the Edison NJ, OM Creativity Site at http://mars.superlink.net/~lsemel, and is a high school senior. He has been to the World Finals five times, placing second in Which End is Up? He plans on going to NYU next year to study film and video.

James Flannigan developed and maintains the University of Texas OM site and the Texas OM site. He's been involved with OM for 7 years, being a founding member in three teams. He's starting a Div. IV team at UT, where he's a freshman, studying Management Information Systems.

If you don't send us e-mail, we'll never know how many people are using the site! That is, unless you are prepared to donate a Web server with CGI access.

Coming to College Park

The Worlds Finals are coming up in early June in College Park, Maryland. We've put together some interesting sites for all of you who will be at the worlds or are just interested.

Information about Maryland and College Park

  • Visit the Maryland Office of Tourism Development.
  • While you're in the area you might as well take a visit to Washington D.C., it's only eight miles by car or a forty minute ride on the Metro Rail which runs right into College Park.
  • Find out about our host, the University of Maryland, College Park.
  • Take a look at what Hot Wired's The Rough Guide has to say about our host state.

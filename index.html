What's This All About?
Odyssey of the Mind is an organization that promotes creative problem solving, and sponsors a worldwide competition in which over one million participate. More information can be found on the www.odysseyofthemind.com. Odyssey of the Mind was featured on television's Creativity with Bill Moyers and I Leonardo, a Journey of the Mind.
